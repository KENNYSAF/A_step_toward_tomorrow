About Me

My name is Artem Filippov. I was born in 2003 in Buryatia, Russia, and grew up between Ukraine and Russia. Life was not easy: I worked on farms with my grandparents, then in kitchens, and later faced the challenges of war. In 2022, I was forced to leave everything behind. My journey took me through Turkey, Moldova, and Germany until I finally reached Canada. Here, I started over from nothing ‚Äî working as a room attendant, houseperson, and banquet server. Every step of my path is a reminder that resilience, faith, and hard work can rebuild a life from the ground up.

My Mission

This is not a registered non-profit. This is my personal initiative, born from my own struggles and my own victories. My mission is to support my family and to bring hope to others who face hardship. I want to show that even one person can rise again, turn pain into strength, and inspire others to believe in tomorrow. The project is simple: sincerity, kindness, responsibility, and the belief that no effort is wasted when it is done with heart.
